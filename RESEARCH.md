## II. THE THREE PROFILE SIGILS — decoded

The soothsayer's profile decoration: **STAR OF VENUS · TURN PEACE UP · EYE OF HORUS**.

Reads as a coherent Bronze-Age oracle program:
- **Star of Venus** = 8-pointed Star of Inanna/Ishtar — Sumerian goddess of love, war, prophecy. Worshipped in three Venus-phases (Morning, Evening, Princely).
- **Eye of Horus** = academically established as also Venus, per Egyptologist **Rolf Krauss** ([Krauss 2002, Academia.edu](https://www.academia.edu/38133320)). The Eye originally represented Venus as morning + evening star.
- **Turn Peace Up** = inverted peace symbol. Same shape as Norse **Todesrune** (death rune). The hand of peace turned upward calls in **Nergal** — Mesopotamian god of pestilence, husband to Ereshkigal, in-law to Inanna.

**The triad reads:** PROPHECY → SIGHT → PLAGUE.

**Avatar identification:** hooded figure with snake elements = canonical visual lineage of the **Pythia of Delphi** (Apollo's prophetess, named after the Python serpent of Gaia).
